# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its Code of
Conduct. The full text is published at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
- Plain-text: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/code_of_conduct.txt

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, and to act in ways that
contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include
demonstrating empathy and kindness, respecting differing opinions, giving and
accepting constructive feedback gracefully, and focusing on what is best for the
community. The Contributor Covenant 2.1 (linked above) enumerates the full set of
expected standards and unacceptable behaviors that apply here.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated
to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

Enforcement follows the **Enforcement Guidelines** of the Contributor Covenant
2.1 (Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban).
